## Tokenizer and CTC Conventions

- Processor: `facebook/wav2vec2-base`
- Transcript casing: uppercase
- Observed raw characters: ` 'ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ`
- Punctuation: removed except ASCII apostrophe.
- Curly apostrophes/backticks: converted to ASCII apostrophe.
- Word delimiter token: `|`
- Blank/pad token: `<pad>` with ID `0`
- Label padding: `-100` in the CTC data collator.
- REF/HYP WER normalization: uppercase, allowed characters `A-Z`, apostrophe, and spaces; repeated whitespace collapsed.
